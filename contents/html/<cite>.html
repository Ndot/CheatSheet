HTML Citation Element

The HTML <cite> Element represents a reference to a creative work (e.g. a book, a song, a movie, a TV show, a painting, a sculpture, etc.).

It should not include a person's name, not even the author's name.

Attributes :

Global Attributes

Example
<p>The <cite>Mona Lisa</cite> was painted by Leonardo di ser Piero da Vinci.</p>
Result

The Mona Lisa was painted by Leonardo di ser Piero da Vinci.
