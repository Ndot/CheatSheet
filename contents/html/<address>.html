HTML Address element

The HTML <address> element supplies contact information for its nearest <article> or <body> ancestor; in the latter case, it applies to the whole document. Typically this element shows inside a footer tag.

Attributes :

Global Attributes

Example
<address>
You can contact author at <a href="http://www.somedomain.com">Some Domain</a>.<br>
You may also want to visit us:<br>
Mozilla Foundation<br>

Building K<br>

USA
</address>
